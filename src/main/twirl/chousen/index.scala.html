Patch notes
                    v0.4-b12 (16 Aug 2017)

                    * Cards can be dropped at a camp fire


                    v0.4-b11 (15 Aug 2017)

                    * Three class/deck choices: Fighter, Berserker, and Warrior
                    * Each deck plays differently using different cards
                    * Destruction now uses status effects when calculating Vit loss
                    * Destruction attack cost decreased to 110 from 130
                    * Stunning Strike charges reduced to 3 from 4
                    * Status effects simplified in UI


                    v0.4-b10 (15 Aug 2017)

                    * New Potion Cards: Potion of Regeneration, Potion of Continuation
                    * Potion of Rage duration increased to 8 from 6
                    * Potion of Rage now also gives bonus Dexterity


                    v0.4-b9 (14 Aug 2017)

                    * Attack skill cards now have up to 4 charges and consume a charge on use
                    * Restored cards will have 1 charge
                    * If the player exhausts their deck, all charges are restored
                    * Halved the number of attack skills in the default deck
                    * StoneSkin and Rage instances reduced from 4 to 2


                    v0.4-b8 (14 Aug 2017)

                    * Support for equipment cards
                    * 4 Equipment cards added to the Player's deck
                    * Player base stats reduced from 8 to 6
                    * Player initial health reduced from 100 to 70
                    * Increased the stats of various enemies
                    * Enemy damage text is more descriptive


                    v0.4-b7 (12 Aug 2017)

                    * RarePepe Card now gives bonus experience, based on player level
                    * Possible to gain more than one level per turn (will occur when using Rare Pepe at low levels)
                    * Experience curve is now exponential


                    v0.4.b6 (2 Aug 2017)

                    * Steam Golem charges up less often
                    * Increased base stats for many enemies


                    v0.4.b5 (2 Aug 2007)

                    * Enemy status effects displayed in UI
                    * Ground Slam now applies a small slow
                    * Fixed steam golem, would never speed up


                    v0.4.b4 (1 Aug 2007)

                    * Added new Status/Potion card: Rage. Increases Strength, Damage, and Speed,
                      but disables the use of any other cards.
                    * Status effects can now apply to enemies
                    * Stunning Strike/Hamstring merged into a single skill.
                    * Stunning Strike now reduces position and applies a stronger, but temporary slow.
                    * Warrior, Steam Golem, and Orcs have interesting alternative actions in battle
                    * Made later enemies hit harder (Old Orc was doing no damage)
                    * It is no-longer possible to attack the Camp Fire


                    v0.4.b3 (31 Jul 2007)

                    * New damage formula.
                    * Dexterity is now half as effective as strength at increasing max damage
                    * Dexterity increases minimum damage dealt at the same rate
                    * Potions of Strength and Stoneskin have additional battle effects
                    * The player now has the ability to block instead of attacking.


                    v0.4.b2 (30 Jul 2007)

                    * Reduced deck and card choices to only Strength based decks
                    * Focus is on getting strength working and fun first, before exploring other stat builds


                    v0.4.b2 (29 Jul 2007)

                    * Support for temporary status effects for the Player
                    * Added new status effecting potions: Potion of Haste, Might, Vitality, etc.


                    v0.4.b1 (29 Jul 2007)

                    * At campfires the player can now choose to:
                        Rest - Heal all missing health
                        Explore - Fill their hand, drawing a minimum of 2 cards
                        Rest and Explore - Heal a small amount and draw a single card


                    v0.3 Carlonean Online (26 Jul 2017)

                    * Many new abilities added
                    * Elements of random generation added to the Dungeon
                    * Basic systems created for classes, decks, leveling
                    * Enhanced development UI


                    v0.2 CQuest Online (24 May 2017)

                    * Website based UI
                    * Redeveloped as a webservice


                    v0.1 CQuest (17 Oct 2016)

                    * Basic turn-based battle engine and sample dungeon
                    * Console based game
